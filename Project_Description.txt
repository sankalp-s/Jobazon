AMZON JOB DATA-SET 

USED Amazon Job Dataset which is collected using Selenium and BeautifulSoup by scraping all of the jobs for Amazon job site. (https://amazon.jobs)
It is a dataset including information on amazon job opening around the world from June 2011 to March 2018. 

By analysing this dataset,I Found out many interesting insights such as -
- number of job openings in a specific location(BANGALORE,INDIA AND SEATTLE,USA)
- jobs available for a specific qualification
- Most popular skillset 
- and many other things
